Making a match with fingerprint scanning

Fingerprints can provide key evidence in catching and convicting criminals. With the advent of new fingerprint scanning technology, the Florida Department of Law Enforcement needed a way to train its large workforce quickly and effectively. Our interactive CD provided clear instruction to law enforcement officers on using the technology, training them to digitally scan and transmit prints over an electronic network.
