Training health care workers

To help ensure the proper training of individuals who will influence the health and welfare of the citizens of Florida, we worked with the Florida Department of Education to develop instructional modules for health science education. A curriculum guide for patient simulator scenarios highlighted the latest advances in medical equipment and technology. By aligning lessons and activities with simulated situations, this training helped health care workers transition from the classroom to the clinic.
