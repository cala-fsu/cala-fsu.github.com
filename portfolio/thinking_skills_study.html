Assessing critical thinking skills

For the Florida Department of Education (FLDOE), we developed a definition and conceptual framework for teaching and assessing higher order thinking skills. Our research, which detailed the theories, teaching strategies, and assessment related to these skills, helped the FLDOE to develop consistent statewide instruments to assess the thinking skills for students at all levels.
