Ensuring quality control of high-stakes tests

In today’s era of accountability, students’ standardized test scores are used to determine student retention, possible school sanctions, and federal funding. For years, CALA has worked with the Florida Department of Education (FLDOE) to provide quality control to ensure scoring accuracy for the Florida Comprehensive Assessment Test.

Our software engineers maintain complex programs to replicate and verify the accuracy of scoring and of the student, school, district, and statewide reports provided by testing vendors each year. We also replicate the calculation of school grades for statewide mandates and Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP) to meet the mandates of No Child Left Behind.
