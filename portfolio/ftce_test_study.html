Better Tests, Better Teachers

FTCE Test Development

Research reveals that an effective teacher is key to student achievement, and at CALA, we want to be a part of bringing top-quality teachers into the classroom. We developed valid and reliable test items for teacher certification examinations with the goal that the examinations truly measure what is expected of a beginning teacher.

The Challenge

CALA was commissioned by the Florida Department of Education (FLDOE) to develop test items for seven Florida Teacher Certification Examinations (FTCE), from initial test developer training to revision of the test information guide, with numerous steps in between.

A more abstract undertaking was to increase the cognitive demand of individual test items—to create questions that test examineesâ€™ capabilities at different levels. Each item must test a particular skill that reflects knowledge of a subject area. Items had to be accurate, unbiased, and current, while containing information that would remain relevant over a five-year period.

The Solution

To complete this complex project, we turned to our diverse talents in training, test development, writing, editing, graphic and web design, marketing, and software engineering.

Our measurement specialists assembled committees of subject-matter experts from across the state and trained them to revise, develop, and validate test items. Working with these experts, we revised and developed documents such as item specifications, competencies and skills, and test blueprints. At any time, FLDOE could participate in test development meetings from a remote location using web meeting software.

We used test bank software for the bank of items. Measurement editors reviewed all test items and related documents for accuracy and adherence to measurement standards.

Because new and revised test items must be pilot tested at centers that represent all regions of Florida, we coordinated with college and university testing centers to participate in Internet-based pilot testing. We developed marketing materials for the centers to publicize the testing opportunities to students. To prepare for the testing, we built a secure website and developed an online version of each examination, making sure that the test published properly and the data were captured. We created an instruction manual to train testing center proctors and provided onsite training to ensure that all procedures and computer requirements were met for test bank security and data capture.

We redesigned test information guides to make them more user-friendly, guaranteeing that the sample test items in each guide reflected the examination in types and difficulty. We also developed online accessible versions to be incorporated into the FLDOE website.

The Results

We succeeded in meeting the creative, technical, and logistical demands of the project. For the pilot testing, we created a secure platform for publishing the tests online and developed an application for locking down the computers at testing centers across the state.

The test items we developed have been incorporated into the FLDOE test banks and are now a part of the FTCE testing cycle. Current examinations will benefit from this infusion of new, rigorous, and relevant items.
