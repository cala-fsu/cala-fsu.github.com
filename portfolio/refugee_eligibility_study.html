For Greater Opportunity

Refugee Eligibility Training

As part of a nationwide naturalization initiative to encourage citizenship, we delivered refugee eligibility training in four languages—English, Spanish, Haitian-Creole, and Serbo-Croatian.

The Challenge

Our task was to develop and conduct a statewide multimedia campaign for the Florida Department of Children and Families to encourage eligible individuals to become United States citizens. The key challenge was to create multilingual materials that would guide immigrants step-by-step through the naturalization process.

The Solution

Other states adapted our multimedia materials for use in their naturalization offices.

We assessed needs, developed a multimedia marketing campaign, and designed instructional materials. These materials included a guidebook to the naturalization process, public service announcements, posters, and postcards. The Spanish, Haitian-Creole, and Serbo-Croatian materials were field tested with the target audiences to ensure the appropriateness of the content and accuracy of translation. Once all of the materials were completed, we provided training to personnel in Floridaâ€™s naturalization offices.

The Results

Our effective and user-friendly training materials helped aspiring citizens with the application process and preparation for the citizenship test and Immigration and Naturalization Service interview. In guiding the individuals toward naturalization, we helped them secure the rights and responsibilities of U.S. citizens. Other states adapted our multimedia materials for use in their naturalization offices. The project was extended to develop orientation training for refugees.
