Addressing the needs of diverse learners

As part of research and development for the Florida Department of Education, we created interactive electronic versions of four test information guides for the Florida Teacher Certification Examinations. We addressed the needs of individual learners who would access the guides and provided multisensory learning experiences for the various learning modalities and accessible versions with similar experiences for those with disabilities. The guides were field-tested by content experts, teachers, and persons with disabilities and commended for easy accessibility and attention to detail for diverse learners. The templates that were produced could be applied to other subject-area test information guides with minimum cost and effort.
