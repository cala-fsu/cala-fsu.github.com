Measuring school readiness

We worked with the Florida Partnership for School Readiness to conduct research on existing test instruments that address school readiness in young children. The evaluation plan outlined recommendations for the development of outcome instruments, uniform screening instruments, and a plan for sampling, data management, and data collection. The evaluation plan also recommended the type of data to be collected at each point in the evaluation process.
