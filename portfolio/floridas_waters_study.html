Protecting Florida’s waters

As part of a multiyear contract, we worked with the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FLDEP) to develop surface water quality status and assessment reports for presentation in print, CD, and Web-based formats. We created and hosted an FTP site for the secure transfer and storage of files.

We compiled an enormous amount of information—narrative text, charts, and graphs from five water basins across Florida—into an engaging format. We also ensured the accuracy of hundreds of detailed figures and maps. In producing these reports, we helped FLDEP in its goal to protect Florida waters and all the life that depends on them.
